Some random things I love about my girlfriend

Lovingly made by Rafael.

She is curious

Everytime we're outside, and we see something interesting, she takes the time to carefully look at it with wonder.

She is cute

Her childlike outlook of the world never fails to bring a smile to my face.

She brings light to my world

Like sunflowers, she is the symbol of warmth and light. Billions of years from now, people will look at the sky and see the vibrant marriage of our Milky Way Galaxy and the Andromeda Galaxy. It will look spectacular and awesome. But one thing they will miss is this beautiful ray of Sunshine living here in the present.

She loves me

Her love is as beautiful as the break of dawn. With each day that passes, I see why God sent her to me. And I love her with all my heart.
